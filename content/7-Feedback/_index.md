>Personal feedback on the experiences during the First Cloud Journey program based on the following categories:

### General Assessment

**1. Work Environment** 
The work environment is extremely dynamic. Whether working at the office or from home (WFH), whenever I have a question, everyone is always ready to support and answer enthusiastically. The office space is neat, cool, and quiet enough to help me focus highly on my work. The point I desire is that the company could organize more networking sessions and increase the number of approved days to the office so that interns have the opportunity to bond.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin** 
I am very impressed with the support from the mentor and team admin. The team admin works extremely thoughtfully, promptly assisting with procedures, documents, and always updating information in a timely manner so I can work smoothly. As for the mentor, they guide very detailedly, explain clearly, and always encourage me to be proactive. I highly appreciate that the mentor does not immediately give the answer but allows me to experiment and troubleshoot on my own. At the same time, being free to choose the pace and learning content according to personal desires helps me not feel constrained and maximizes my self-study ability.

**3. Relevance between Work and Major** 
At first, I thought that knowledge about Cloud infrastructure might not be very related to my Cybersecurity major orientation. However, when delving into network architecture and AWS services, I realized the compatibility is extremely high. The work not only helps me consolidate foundational knowledge about system security but also broadens my vision to new areas (like Cloud-Native, Serverless) that I had never been exposed to in university.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development** 
Throughout the internship, I have absorbed a massive amount of knowledge. Not stopping at specialized theory, I learned how to deploy a practical Cloud system from scratch. "Learning goes hand in hand with practice" helped me rub shoulders and mature a lot. Moreover, observing the professionalism in a corporate environment and the practical experience shared by the mentor helped me have a clearer orientation for my upcoming career path.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit** 
The company culture always maintains a joyful and sociable vibe, but when it comes to work, it is extremely serious and professional. Regarding team spirit, our group divided the work very reasonably based on each person's strengths, and everyone always strived to complete their part well. Colleagues at the internship site also supported each other very positively, creating a feeling of bonding like a real collective even though I am just an intern.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns** 
The company provides very good conditions regarding time, helping me flexibly balance study and work. Instructional materials are provided in detail. Especially, being able to participate in internal training sessions and events is a big plus, helping me absorb many new ideas and in-depth knowledge from experts.

---

### Other Questions

* **What are you most satisfied with during the internship?** 
That is the professional work environment, enthusiastic support from the mentor and admins, and especially the opportunity to directly engage with Cloud services in practice to accumulate valuable real-world experience.

* **What do you think the company needs to improve for future interns?** 
I think the company should optimize the registration system so that people can be approved to go to the office for more sessions. In addition, internal instructional materials and videos need to be updated, as the AWS Console interface and current service features have changed significantly compared to the old versions.

* **If introducing to friends, would you recommend them to intern here? Why?** 
Definitely YES. Because the environment here is very good and highly practical. Going through this internship, you will accumulate a large amount of useful experience and skills, creating a solid stepping stone for future development in the tech industry.

---

### Proposals & Desires

* **Do you have any suggestions to improve the experience during the internship?** 
As mentioned above, I only suggest updating the instructional materials and videos to closely match the current AWS interface. In general, my overall experience throughout the program has been wonderful.

* **Do you want to continue this program in the future?** 
Yes, very willing if I feel there is an opportunity.
